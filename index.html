Our work

A look at what's shipped and what's cooking. Build-in-public by default. We show the wins and the iterations.

WEB APP + APILive

Netropy

Tachograph estimator for drivers and fleet managers. Calculates legal driving and rest times in milliseconds and keeps fleets compliant with EU drivers' hours rules.

< 1 sec saved per store
The problem

EU drivers' hours and tachograph rules are strict, and getting them wrong means fines and failed inspections. Fleets were estimating legal driving and rest time by gut or in spreadsheets.

What we built

A web app that calculates legal driving and rest times in real time, plus an API that drops the same compliance checks straight into other transport software.

Live now

Netropy is shipped and in use, web app and API. Not a demo. Go use it and judge us on the real thing.
